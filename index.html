About This Game

Snake 2077 redefines what it means to be a snake in the modern gaming landscape. With over 200 hours of content, an always-online world that remembers every choice you make, and a dynamic weather system that affects nothing, this is the snake game you never knew you needed.

Key Features:
• 8K textures (sold separately)
• Real-time ray tracing (RTX 4090 required)
• Dynamic AI that adapts to your playstyle (by making it harder)
• 47 DLCs including "The ability to pause" ($2.99/month)
• Mandatory 3-hour tutorial that cannot be skipped
• Cloud saves (limited to 1 save slot, additional slots $0.99 each)

⚠️ Online connection required at all times. Singlemode gameplay is considered a bug and will be patched.
